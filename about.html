O U R   H I S T O R Y

When DELUXE first opened its doors in 1985, this exquisite tower in the heart of Sri Lanka was described as a “Riviera in the Island”

Reflecting the vision of hotel DELUXE through the eyes of interior designer George Hemsworth, This is a “curated mansion” in the heart of the vibrant Colombo district that blends architectural creativity, cultural diversity and timeless elegance. Thoughtfully-designed accommodations featuring 110 rooms and suites are complemented by East-meets-West elements that provide the perfect setting to offering personalised DELUXE Butler Service. The hotel features one-MICHELIN-starred L’Envol French restaurant helmed by Chef Olivier Elzer and one-MICHELIN-starred Rùn Chinese restaurant led by Chef Hung Chi-Kwong, while The Drawing Room hosts a redefined Afternoon Tea experience and offers all-day international dining, and The DELUXE Bar signature cocktails are inspired by the tales of two cities - Colombo and New York.

DE SPA

De spa opens into a blooming garden dedicate to well-being and balanced offering a sloarium & open-air treatment pavillions.

AIRPORT SHUTTLE SERVICE

24 hours free shuttle service from Bandaranaike International Airport,Colombo to the hotel suite.

GOLF

Our beautiful golf courses, in equally beautiful settings, are as much fun for novices to play as they are for the pros.

INDOOR & OUTDOOR POOLS

A paragon-decorated with spanish majolica tiles and supported by a sturdy system used by railroad bridges.Built in 1965. This is one of the oldest in Colombo, One of the biggest in the country & perhaps the most beautiful in the world.

FITNESS CENTER

Our newly renovated, three level fitness center features cardio machines, cable machines, free-weight stations, peloton bags and more.

DELUXE BAR

Enjoy an intimate aperitif in the library, relax on the terrace or pull up a stool to watch our bartenders craft champagne coktails and pour legendary single malts.

G O L F   C O U R S E

Hotel DELUXE was purchased from Diageo by Ennismore, a London-based owner and developer of unique hospitality properties and experiences. The DELUXE Golf Arena, a new 2,500 sq m events space and golf course, was officially opened.

N E W   S H A P E

Ennismore launched a four-year renovation programme at DELUXE, the biggest investment in the hotel’s history, marking the start of an exciting new chapter. The transformation has seen the creation of three new bars, three restaurants, an afternoon tea lounge, children’s adventure spaces, a beauty lodge, and the redesign of all bedrooms and suites.
